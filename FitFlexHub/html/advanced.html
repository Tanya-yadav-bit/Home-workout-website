Step-by-Step Guide

1. Dynamic Warm-Up

Perform dynamic stretches like high knees, butt kicks, and arm swings for 5 minutes.

2. High-Intensity Exercises

Include burpees, mountain climbers, and jump squats. Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, followed by a 15-second rest. Repeat for 3-4 rounds.

3. Advanced Cool Down

End with a 5-minute deep stretching routine targeting all major muscle groups.
