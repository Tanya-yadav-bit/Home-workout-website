Morning Yoga for Beginners

A gentle yoga routine to help you start your day with energy and positivity.

10-Minute Meditation

A quick and simple meditation practice to calm your mind and improve focus.

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Start with Meditation

Find a quiet spot and sit comfortably. Close your eyes, take deep breaths, and focus on your breathing for 5-10 minutes.

2. Gentle Yoga Poses

Perform poses like Child's Pose, Cat-Cow Stretch, and Downward Dog. Hold each pose for 20-30 seconds while breathing deeply.

3. Cool Down

End your session with a few minutes of deep breathing or a relaxing pose like Savasana to fully relax your body and mind.
